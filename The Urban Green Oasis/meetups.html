Community Meetups: 100 Events Annually!

Join The Urban Green Oasis community for exciting, educational, and inspiring meetups throughout the year. We aim to host **100 meetups annually** to connect urban gardeners, share knowledge, and foster a thriving green community!

Our Meetup Philosophy

We believe in the power of shared experiences and collective learning. Our meetups are designed to be:

  • Diverse: Covering a wide range of topics from beginner basics to advanced techniques.
  • Accessible: Held in various formats (online webinars, in-person workshops, garden tours) and locations.
  • Engaging: Interactive sessions, Q&As, and hands-on activities.
  • Community-Driven: Many ideas and even hosting duties come from our active members!
